About us

We are a non-profit charitable trust established in 1970 formed to tackle the problems of poverty in areas around Mumbai.
Our Motto is

Mankind's Service is service to God

During this Corona pandemic, We provided the monetary help to migrant workers who were stuck in Mumbai. We are taking care of stray dogs by feeding them. We have prodvided rations to the peoples. We distributed the notebooks to school children as schools have started with the online teaching

We are grateful to the Trusts,Firms, Institutions and Individuals who have extended their helping hands from time to time and thus encouraged us to go ahead in our charitable activities.
